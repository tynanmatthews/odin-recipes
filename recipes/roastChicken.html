Roast Chicken

Description

This succulent roast chicken is a must addition to your weekly routine. I find it absolutely to die for and good news for you gym junkies out there, it's super calorie dense!

Ingredients

  • 2 Carrots
  • 2kg Whole Chook
  • 2 Brown Onions
  • 1 Lemon
  • 2 Stalks of Celery
  • Your favourite Spices

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees Celcius
  2. Chop vegetables into quarters and add to tray
  3. Place chicken on top of vegetables, dry with a paper towel and season
  4. Stab lemon with a sharp knife all over and shove it in the chooks cavity. Whack some herbs in here if you fancy
  5. Place tray into oven, reduce heat to 200, let cook for 1 hour and 25 minutes
  6. Allow chicken to rest for 10 minutes before carving
